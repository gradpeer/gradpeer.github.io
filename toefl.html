What we offer for TOEFL?

With TOEFL being designed to test the candidates on a diverse set of paradigms, TOEFL Peer focuses on a wholesome development of your command over English language.

With a personalized assessment, we provide you with a broad set of practice problems for each section of the TOEFL exam - Reading, Speaking, Listening and Writing. We also provide strategic tips and a structural framework to excel in each of these sections, keeping in mind the time constraints during the exam.
