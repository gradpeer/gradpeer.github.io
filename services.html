GRE Peer

At GradPeer, we provide you with a systematic plan for everything you need in your GRE preparation. Being your GRE Peer, we provide you with our exhaustive material, tests, online consulting and personalized feedback sessions from experts and high-scorers.

We design a methodical and utilitarian approach to help you attain top scores in both Quant and Verbal sections. We also provide one-on-one assessment for enhancing your AWA score.

TOEFL Peer

With TOEFL being designed to test the candidates on a diverse set of paradigms, TOEFL Peer focuses on a wholesome development of your command over English language.

With a personalized assessment, we provide you with a broad set of practice problems for each section of the TOEFL exam - Reading, Speaking, Listening and Writing. We also provide strategic tips and a structural framework to excel in each of these sections, keeping in mind the time constraints during the exam.

SOP Peer

Your Statement of Purpose defines your personality and helps you establish a relationship with the reviewer. The right content, structure and storyline can call attention to your strengths, account for your weaknesses and set you apart. We are here to help you add all this and more to turn your essay into a winning SOP.

With SOP Peer, we provide you with a detailed feedback for your essay and also provide one-on-one sessions for specific concerns and reviews. We also put special emphasis on maintaining your privacy - your confidentiality is our priority!
