Get to know about our special packages

We provide a number of packages depending on the amount of preparation aid you require and the preparation time at hand. These packages include services like GRE / TOEFL preparation material, sample tests, resume and SOP editing by our experts, completely personalized preparation schedule, and interactive doubt clearing sessions.

Our packages start at as low as $14.99 annually. In cases you would want to subscribe for individual services instead of complete packages, please visit our services page.
